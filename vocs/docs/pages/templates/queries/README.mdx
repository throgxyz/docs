## Queries

Read-only access to the chain and local helpers: inspect accounts and blocks,
convert address formats, do TRX/sun math, connect to a custom endpoint, and
list witnesses and governance proposals. None require a private key.

- [Connect to a custom endpoint](/examples/queries/connect_custom)
- [List witnesses (SRs)](/examples/queries/list_witnesses)
- [List governance proposals](/examples/queries/governance_list)
